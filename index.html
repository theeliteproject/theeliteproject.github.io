Dan Brandt

Elite Project Consulting, nadsdabs & Jara Elements 

Dan Brandt holds two degrees in the biological sciences and has 17 years of experience working for two Fortune 100 pharmaceutical companies. In his early years, Dan was an R& D technician in a research and development pilot plant and worked on scale-up and validation processes of active pharmaceutical ingredients and intermediates. Nine years later, he took a position in R& D Quality Assurance with a focus on document management, change control, and personnel training. In 2014 Dan left the pharmaceutical industry to consult within the medical cannabis industry in Colorado and offered quality services such as designing internal quality systems, writing policies and procedures, and creating personnel training plans. He has also consulted on formulation of hemp CBD infused products and has been an integral part in starting several companies and contributing to the success of others. Dan currently operates a laboratory that focuses strictly on hemp CBD infused product development and pilot scale white label manufacturing. Innovation and maintaining a fresh perspective in this ever-competitive space is a core philosophy that gives his clients the best advantages possible.
