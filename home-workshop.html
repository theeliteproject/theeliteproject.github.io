What are "Home Workshops"?

The Elite Project Home Workshops are intended to share knowledge with the public to empower individuals with the skills to be self-sustainable when it comes to cannabis and hemp. These hands-on events will cover three subjects; growing, extracting, and infusions. Each subject has multiple topics to split the content up so it can be presented in an effective way.

The workshops will address hemp and cannabis as one and discuss differences if appropriate. We understand that some may want to, or are only able to, work with one or the other, and for the most part these processes work for both.

Who are "Home Workshops" for?

Growers

Whether you're just starting out on your growing journey or would like to brush up on your skills, our Home Workshops is designed just for you. Learn proven techniques in growth, tricks for managing pests, and how to use nutrients correctly and effectively,

Extractors

Extraction is a basic idea but a tricky process. With multiple ways to achieve concentration, we take the guesswork out of it. Our Home Workshops cover how to make extracts safely, going in-depth to the actual refinement processes and the basics for creating bases and butters.

Processors

Making a quality product that will illicit the specific effects you're looking for is harder than mixing a few ingredients and giving her a shake! Our Home Workshop will teach you the math and how to apply it to making infused foods, tinctures, topicals.

Why we started "Home Workshops"!

With the current retail prices of products, we feel it’s important to become self-sustainable, especially if you’re managing conditions that require a large amount of product. Even if you don’t intend to grow, extract, or make infused products at home, you’ll never look at retail the same after gaining this knowledge. Learn how to read labels, what it takes to make products, and how the type of product will determine the outcome of its effectivity.

We've found that the demand for this kind of information is huge and realized that it NEEDED to be readily available for people. Due to the huge demand for our workshops we have scheduled them to stagger for starting times every 2 months with only 12 seats per class.
